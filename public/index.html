CHANGE IN WORLD CURRENCIES AFTER BREXIT

The British Pound fell significantly after the referendum vote. Each currency appreciated against the pound by an average of 6.89%. Currencies have increased in value by an average of 13.26% as of today.

CHANGE IN AVIATION COMPANIES' SHARE PRICE

British airline companies have seen their shares fall large percentages on fears about a drop-off in UK travel abroad.

VISITS TO THE UK BY TYPE
